# Prompts para ChatGPT Image — Character Sheets Garden AI Hosts

Cada prompt genera un character sheet visual tipo "concept art board" con múltiples ángulos/expresiones del personaje + datos clave + paleta de colores + vestuario.

---

## PROMPT 1: VALENTINA FRANCO — Lifestyle & Design Host

```
Create a professional character sheet / reference board for an AI-generated female automotive host character named "VALENTINA FRANCO" for a premium automotive brand content series.

CHARACTER DETAILS:
- 31 years old Paraguayan woman
- Height: 1.68m, slim-athletic build (does yoga)
- Hair: Dark brown, shoulder-length, naturally wavy. Sometimes in a messy bun
- Eyes: Dark brown, very expressive
- Skin: Light olive / warm tan, typical Latin American complexion, subtle freckles across nose
- Face: Defined jawline, wide warm smile, photogenic
- Overall vibe: Effortlessly sophisticated — always looks good without trying

LAYOUT — show these views on the sheet:
1. MAIN PORTRAIT (center-left, largest): 3/4 view, slight smile, confident posture. Wearing beige oversized blazer over white crew-neck tee, straight-leg jeans. Standing next to a car, one hand touching the roof. Golden hour lighting.
2. CLOSE-UP FACE (top right): Direct eye contact, warm expression. Detail of freckles and expressive eyes.
3. WORKING POSE (bottom left): Inside a car, touching the dashboard materials with one hand, looking analytically at the interior. Blazer off, just the white tee. Professional but casual.
4. FULL BODY STANDING (right): Relaxed pose, hands in blazer pockets, minimal white sneakers visible. Clean background.
5. EXPRESSION STUDIES (small, bottom right): 3 small head shots showing: (a) analyzing something seriously, (b) laughing naturally, (c) explaining to camera with hand gesture.

STYLE & ART DIRECTION:
- Photorealistic, cinematic quality, soft natural lighting
- Color palette strip at the bottom: beige #D4C5B2, warm gray #9E9590, terracotta #C4703F, cream #F5F0EB, black #1A1A1A, Garden red #DA291C as accent
- Dark charcoal background (#111111) for the sheet
- Typography: clean sans-serif labels in white
- Include text labels: "VALENTINA FRANCO" (large, top), "Age: 31 | Lifestyle & Design Host | Asunción, PY" (smaller), segment names "Por Dentro • Diseño en 60s • Vivirlo"
- Bottom corner: small red square logo with "Garden" in white + "AI HOST 01"
- Professional concept art / character design board aesthetic, NOT anime or cartoon
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 landscape
```

## NOTAS DE USO

### Consistencia entre los tres
- Los tres comparten: fondo #111111, tipografía sans-serif blanca, logo Garden rojo en esquina, layout general similar
- Se diferencian por: paleta de colores, iluminación (cálida→dramática→fría), vestuario, energía general
- Si se ponen los tres character sheets lado a lado deben leerse como un equipo cohesivo pero con personalidades distintas

### Para iterar
- Si la cara no queda bien, agregar al prompt: "Maintain exact same facial features across all views — this is a character consistency sheet"
- Si queda muy cartoon: agregar "Hyper-photorealistic, shot on Sony A7IV, 85mm f/1.4 lens quality"
- Si la ropa no es correcta: especificar más marcas de referencia de estilo (no logos, sino siluetas)
- Para mantener consistencia entre los 3: generar el primero, y en los siguientes agregar "Same artistic style and layout as the Valentina Franco character sheet previously generated"

### Resolución
- Pedir "high resolution, 4K quality, detailed textures" si la resolución no es suficiente
- 16:9 landscape para que funcione como slide de presentación o como asset imprimible
